SOC Log Investigation - Commands Used

1. Identify failed login attempts:
grep "Failed password" OpenSSH_2k.log

2. Identify top attacking IP's:
grep "Failed password" OpenSSH_2k.log | awk '{print $(NF-3)}' | sort | uniq -c | sort -nr | head

3. Identify targeted usernames:
grep "Failed password" OpenSSH_2k.log | awk '{print $(NF-5)}' | sort | uniq -c | sort -nr | header

4. Detect successful logins:
grep "Accepted password" OpenSSH_2k.log

Investigate compromised account:
grep "ftzu" OpenSSH_2k.log
